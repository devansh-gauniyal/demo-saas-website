# Launch checklist for small SaaS teams

Use this checklist before shipping a new feature, pricing change, or product
announcement.

<img src="/assets/launch-calendar.svg" />

## Before launch

- Confirm the target audience.
- Write the main message.
- Prepare homepage, pricing, email, social, and sales assets.
- Decide who approves each asset.

<img src="/assets/channel-board.svg" />

## After launch

Review traffic, signups, conversions, and customer replies within 48 hours.
